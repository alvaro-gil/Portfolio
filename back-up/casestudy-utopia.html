Event Microsite

Utopia Music Festival

Project Brief

The goal was to create an event and design a microsite with a unique feature that will bring added value to my user.

As a lover of electronic music, I began by choosing an Electronic Dance Music (EDM) Festival as the type of event.

Sprint Length

4 Days

Roles in UCD Process

Research, Interaction Design, Branding, & UI Design

User Research

Diving into research, I conducted five user interviews focusing on the things people did before and during the music festivals they'd attended.

Insights

What people wanted most from their festival experience was to witness amazing live events alongside their friends. However, certain factors they couldn't get around would occasionally inconvenience them.

I usually go with the intention of gaining a great live experience and sharing a few memories with my friends along the way.

Festival Fabien

24-Year-Old Festival Ethusiast

Goals
  • Watch favorite artists play
  • Make memories with friends
  • Find information about festival
  • Purchase tickets
Pains
  • Limited set information ahead of time
  • Wasting festival time coordinating artist sets
  • Loses track of friends
  • Occasionally sees a very sick person
